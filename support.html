Frequently Asked Questions

How does GlowUp work?

GlowUp uses advanced AI to analyze your selfie across 7 categories: skin quality, facial symmetry, jawline definition, grooming, style, physique, and posture. You receive a detailed score breakdown with personalized improvement recommendations.

Are my photos private?

Yes. Your photos are stored securely and are only accessible to you. They are processed by AI for analysis purposes only. You can delete all your data at any time from the Settings screen.

How accurate are the scores?

Scores are AI-generated and designed for entertainment and self-improvement purposes. They provide a consistent framework for tracking your progress over time. Results are not medical or professional assessments.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription at any time through your Apple ID settings: Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > GlowUp. Subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open GlowUp, go to the upgrade screen, and tap "Restore purchases" at the bottom. This will restore any active subscriptions linked to your Apple ID.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll to the bottom, and tap "Delete Account." This permanently deletes all your data including photos, analyses, chat history, and profile information.

Why did my score change?

Scores can vary slightly based on lighting, angle, and photo quality. For the most consistent results, use good lighting, face the camera straight on, and take photos in similar conditions each time.
