Common Questions

How does the AI face analysis work?

GlowUp uses AI technology to analyze your facial features across categories like symmetry, jawline, skin quality, grooming, and style. Your photo is processed securely and is never stored on our servers.

How do I restore my subscription?

Open GlowUp, go to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom. This will restore any active subscription linked to your Apple ID.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find GlowUp, and tap "Cancel Subscription."

How do I delete my account?

Open GlowUp, go to Settings (gear icon), scroll down, and tap "Delete Account." This will permanently delete all your data including analysis history, profile information, and achievements.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Your photos are processed in real-time by AI and immediately discarded — they are never stored on our servers. Only your analysis scores and text recommendations are saved to your account. You can read our full Privacy Policy for details.
